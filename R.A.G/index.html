Welcome to R.A.G Studios

We at R.A.G studios are a team of aspiring developers. Our studio was established in 2021 by a group of talented developers, designers, animators and engineers whose passion for fun and entertainment led them to take a leap into the unknown.

How we started

What started out as a small group of gaming enthusiasts and developers who just wanted to make a games, but we fast realised we could do so much more. Our studio now consists of artist, engineers, developers, and so much more. Our goal is to create amazing content that we are sure to blow your minds, and most importantly have fun while doing it.
